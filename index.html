F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What can the Stash & Hunt Platform do?

    It is an ecosystem that will help corporate to create their knowledge base. As soon as a company grows its knowledge base it helps their employee to get the right answer at their fingers tip. It is a "Private search engine for corporate", using this platform any employee can log in and can search the query they have. It is powered with AI/ML that helps the platform to understand the question, search the relevant answer and let the user know about it.

  • How are Stash & Hunt is different from Google?

    Whenever we ask any question to Google, it fetches the data available on WWW and brings it back on the user screen whereas Stash & Hunt is a private search engine exclusively or the corporate that means a corporate will save their content and any employee of that firm can ask the question to Stash & Hunt the way we are asking today to Google.

  • How do I collaborate?

    You can create content and make it available to the public workspace. Once your content is live then your peer can search the content, give a review, comment, and share.

  • What is a private workspace?

    While making the content you can save it in your private workspace, by this way you can only search the content, not your peers.

  • Can I edit the content?

    Yes, you can alter your content, delete your content, change workspace, distribution list and many more.

  • How secure is my content?

    Very much. One company’s information can’t be accessible to others. We do store the data of each customer in the offline server which is not connected over the Internet to avoiding hacking in the first place. And, on top of it we are using encryption at rest with AES-256, one of the strongest encryption methods available.

  • Does Stash & Hunt support communication from other platforms?

    Yes, it has a facility to sync with other application data, so users no need to navigate from one application to other to get their query resolution.

  • What hosting options does Stash & Hunt provide?

    There are two: 1 - You can host with us. We are responsible for security. 2 - You can deploy on your cloud or corporate data center.

  • Why should I use Stash & Hunt?

    You can save your content and later its AI-enabled web application will help you to get the right content as per the query asked by you. You can also, get your query resolution by other contents available in it.
